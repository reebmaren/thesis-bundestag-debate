Evaluation results

The data has been filtered by fraction in order to show the change in the expression of the probability values over time. With the help of the visualizations, it is possible to find certain patterns. The topics shown are those that appear most when "Immigration (topic 5)" is the highest proportioned topic. One can see the trend: These topics have lower proportions until the mid-1980s and then increase until the mid-1990s. The graph of the CDU/CSU, for example, shows that the topic "German Unification (topic 21)" has a higher share than that of the SPD prior to 1990. There is also a trend towards higher shares for "Development (topic 3)" at the change of government in 1998 and "European policy (topic 18)" in the early 2000s. These patterns can and should be verified at the source level. The results are always only a first approximation. However, these results are well suited as an exploratory, heuristic means of identifying sources that are potentially relevant to a question in the large number of documents.



Examples of exploratory evaluation of further results

In this project, the entire corpus of Bundestag speeches was given to the model. Therefore, the topics represent a wide range of different political topics. Here are two more topic visualizations. Both of them contain the topic "German Unification (21)". The graph is only slightly distorted in relation to the other lines, and you can see that the share of this topic remains roughly the same over time. For example, the graph on the left shows the trend of a sharp increase in the share of the topic "Employment (25)" in the mid-1990s and then a decline again in the early 2000s. In the early 1990s, following the reunification of East and West Germany, the country faced high unemployment. This was due to the economic challenges associated with integrating the two regions. However, Germany implemented several labor market reforms and economic policies aimed at reducing unemployment starting in the mid-1990s. These reforms became known as the "Hartz reforms." They were implemented in the early 2000s. The model shows a trend without saying anything about the content of the speeches. The graph on the left shows the topic "Foreign Policy (8)". It includes keywords like "afghanistan", "hilfe", "vereint" (see Appendix Thesis). In the mid-2000s, the graph shows an increase in the proportion. Germany's involvement in Afghanistan was part of the international military coalition in the aftermath of the September 11, 2001 terrorist attacks in the United States. The German government pledged its support to the United States in the fight against terrorism. It joined the NATO-led mission in Afghanistan. In April 2021, the German government announced the withdrawal of its remaining troops from Afghanistan. This marks the end of its military mission in the country. The topic is likely to concentrate debates around this military mission.
