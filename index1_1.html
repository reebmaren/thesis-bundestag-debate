More contrasting display of term frequencies

In a further step, the data were filtered according to faction and were also fed into a dictionary that contained the number of counts per year. To establish a content-related relationship between the mentions of all factions and those of SPD and CDU/CSU, the results were converted into a stacked bar plot. In this way it is possible to interpret the ratio of mentions of these groups compared to all groups. It is also possible to compare patterns more easily. Based on the same data results, this presentation is an improvement over the previous one.
